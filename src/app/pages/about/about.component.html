CheesyBite Story

Founded in Muradnagar years ago with a passion for premium artisanal pizza.

Started as a small shop on GT Road, CheesyBite has now become a famous landmark across the region. Our journey began with a simple belief: pizza can be incredibly flavorful and authentic.

Our commitment to quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods sets us apart. We use a secret family recipe, hand-tossing our dough and baking it in a traditional stone oven to ensure every pizza is perfect.

Today, we are proud to be a premium pizza kitchen, serving happiness in every slice to thousands of loyal customers.
